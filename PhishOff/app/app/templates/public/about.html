What is Phish Off and what is the purpose of this web application?

Phish Off is full of informative information and interactive features that will give you everything you will need to know to help yourself be more confident in and around real-life phishing instances. The goal of this Web application is to teach you all about phishing and how to avoid falling victim to attacks. This is done by providing users with a place to practise and learn about Phishing and it's consequences whilst also gaining knowledge on what to look out for and steps to avoid it.

Why Phishing?

Phishing is a profound problem that is often overlooked due to people being unaware of the consequences and not being aware of computer security. Phishing is the easiest attack to create and execute so criminals are everywhere, being aware of the methods that are used to carry out these attacks is essential. If you are victim of a Social engineering attack like Phishing it can be devestating due to the attackers usually striving for financial gain. Most attackers, if not all are ruthless due to it being an anonymous attack that can be incredibly difficult to trace.
